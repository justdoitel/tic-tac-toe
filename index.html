Tic Tac Toe

Basic Rules: Tic Tac Toe is a two-player game where each player takes turns marking a 3x3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the round.

Specific rules

  • You'll play five rounds
  • Player 1 is always X while Player 2 is always O
  • Player 1 would start the first round. However, for the following rounds, the winner of the previous round would begin the game.
  • If a round ends in a draw, the next round (if there is one) would start with the player who didn't start last time.
  • At the end of the fifth round, whoever has the most wins wins the whole game.
  • If the two players are tied, then the game would end in a tie.
