Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a two-player game where each player takes turns marking a 3x3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the round.

At the end of the fifth round, whoever has the most wins wins the whole game. If the two players are tied, then the game would end in a tie.
